Community

At greenDAB, we are deeply committed to enriching our local community by supporting community radio stations such as Maritime Radio and Miskin Radio at North Kent College.

These stations are pivotal in delivering local news, entertainment, and educational content, fostering a sense of community and engagement among residents.

Maritime Radio

On 96.5 FM, Maritime Radio serves the Royal Borough of Greenwich and surrounding areas. The station offers diverse programming, including local news, music, and community updates, ensuring listeners stay informed and connected with their locality. Maritime Radio's dedication to reflecting the interests and concerns of the community makes it an invaluable asset to the region.

Miskin Radio at North Kent College

Miskin Radio, based at North Kent College, provides students and community members with hands-on radio broadcasting experience. The station broadcasts live 24/7, covering West Kent and surrounding areas, and offers a platform for aspiring broadcasters to develop their skills. Miskin Radio contributes significantly to the community's cultural fabric by nurturing new talent and delivering diverse programming.

greenDAB aims to empower local voices, promote media literacy, and strengthen community ties by supporting these community radio stations. Our collaboration with Maritime Radio and Miskin Radio underscores our commitment to fostering a vibrant, informed, and connected community.
