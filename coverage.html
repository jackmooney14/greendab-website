Coverage

greenDAB Limited's digital radio services are strategically designed to reach a substantial audience across South East London and North West Kent.

Our coverage area encompasses key urban centres and surrounding communities, including Greenwich, Bromley, Bexley, Lewisham, Dartford, and Gravesham. This extensive reach ensures that a diverse population can access a wide array of digital radio programming, fostering community engagement and providing a platform for local voices.

Our multiplex infrastructure is optimised to deliver reliable, high-quality digital audio broadcasting, ensuring a consistent listening experience throughout the designated coverage zone.

greenDAB Limited is committed to strengthening the local media landscape and offering a vital service to residents and businesses by focusing on these areas.

We aim to expand digital radio's accessibility, providing a rich and varied selection of stations that reflect the unique character of South East London and North West Kent.
