About Us

GreenDAB Limited is dedicated to providing high-quality digital radio services to South East London and North West Kent.

As a licensed Small-Scale DAB multiplex operator, we are committed to enhancing local broadcasting by offering a platform for diverse and community-focused radio stations.

We aim to foster a vibrant digital radio landscape, ensuring local voices are heard, and listeners can access various programming.

We are passionate about supporting local sound programme services and promoting diversity in broadcasting within our coverage area.
